Meaningful Flare integration

Five modules, cleanly separated.

Escrow, market competition, bond custody, pricing and cross-chain proof verification each have one job.

01 / USDT0

Guaranteed escrow

Holds the buyer's real test stablecoin until proof selects the outcome.

02 / MATCHER

Sealed competition

Two maker wallets commit and reveal domain-separated quotes.

03 / FXRP

Performance bond

The winner's bond is returned on delivery or slashed on default.

04 / FTSOv2

Fair price guard

The winning quote must stay within a 1.25% live XRP/USD band.

05 / FDC

Dual proof settlement

Payment releases escrow; non-payment refunds and compensates the buyer.
