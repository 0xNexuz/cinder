How it works

Four clear steps.

Commit–reveal is the working alternative to the unavailable FCC host. Quotes stay hidden during bidding without a trusted matching service.

01 / LOCK
▣

Buyer funds escrow

cUSD is locked on Coston2 with amount, expiry, destination and price limits.

02 / MATCH
#

Makers commit, then reveal

Each maker posts a quote hash. Prices can only be revealed after bidding closes.

03 / DELIVER
→

Winner sends XRP

The contract chooses the lowest valid quote and creates a unique payment reference.

04 / VERIFY
✓

FDC releases funds

FDC verifies the XRPL payment and the escrow pays the winning maker.
